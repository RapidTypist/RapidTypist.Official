Users can start depositing CFD to KuCoin immediately and trading CFD on KuCoin November 8th at 20:00 (UTC +8).

About Confido Gold:

Confido aims to revolutionise ecommerce shipping by providing an easy to use app that allows buyers and sellers to transact seamlessly while also providing a tracking feature. All secured by blockchain technology.

The app can already be integrated with the following vendors: United States Postal Service, FedEx, UPS, DHL, Postnl and EMS.

Confido works by using smart contracts with a unique shipment tracking feature. When a new transaction is made by a user, Confido will generate a smart contract using a secure smart contract template and the data entered by the user.

That smart contract will act as the escrow between buyer and seller. Once the funds have been received by the smart contract the seller is prompted to submit a shipment tracking code to the smart contract (which is all done inside the app).

Our smart contract will continuously track the delivery status of the package and will release the funds 24 hours after the package has been delivered. In case of a wrong or faulty product the buyer can put the transaction on hold.

They successfully completed their ICO in 40 seconds, $300,000 were collected in this short time. They have clearly become one of the most successful ICOs of 2017.
